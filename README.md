# Ministry Expense Tracker — User Guide

A simple app for tracking ministry expense receipts and mileage, and exporting them for reimbursement. Everything stays on your own phone — nothing is uploaded anywhere, and no account is needed.

## Install (one time, ~1 minute)

1. Open the app link in **Safari** on your iPhone
2. Tap the **Share** button (square with arrow), then **Add to Home Screen**, then **Add**
3. Open it from your home screen like any other app
4. When asked, allow **Camera** (for receipt photos) and **Location** (for mileage and auto-filling city/state) — choose "Allow While Using App"

## Receipts

1. On the **Receipts** tab, tap the photo field to snap a picture of the receipt
2. Enter the amount using digits only — the decimal is automatic: type `2300` for $23.00, or `413` for $4.13
3. Pick a **Category**, then a **Sub-category** (the list narrows to match the category)
4. Fill in vendor and ministry purpose
5. Country/State/City fill automatically from your last entry — tap **📍 Fill from my location** to update them, or use the **✕** buttons to clear and enter manually
6. Tap **Save Receipt**

Tap any saved receipt to view it, **Edit** it, share the photo, mark it exported/not exported, or delete it.

## Mileage

1. On the **Mileage** tab, name the trip and enter your **start odometer** (pre-filled from your last trip)
2. Check the mileage rate (defaults to 72.5¢/mile) and tap **▶ Start Trip**
3. Drive. **Keep the app open on screen** — a dash mount and charger work well. The screen stays awake automatically
4. On arrival tap **■ Stop Trip** — GPS distance and end odometer are calculated; adjust if needed, then **Save**

Tap any saved trip to view, edit (including the date), or delete it.

## Exporting for reimbursement

On the **Export** tab:

- **Export Mileage Log (Excel)** — trip details plus rate and dollar amount, with totals
- **Export Receipt List (Excel)** — all receipt details with category and amount totals
- **Share Receipt Photos** — sends all receipt photos at once

Use the share sheet to **Save to Files** (iCloud Drive) or email straight to your reimbursement contact.

**Green ✔ / Red ✘:** every receipt and trip shows whether it's been exported yet. Exports only include new (✘) items by default — uncheck the "Only new" box to re-export everything. Items are marked ✔ automatically after a successful export. An optional date range filter is at the top.

## Good to know

- **Your data is private.** Everyone using the link gets their own separate copy — no one can see anyone else's receipts
- **Export regularly.** Data lives in your phone's browser storage; saving exports to iCloud Drive is your backup. Don't clear Safari website data for this site without exporting first
- **GPS accuracy** is typically within 1–2% of your odometer, and you can hand-correct any trip afterward
